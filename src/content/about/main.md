title: About me
---

I am a computational astrophysicist studying star clusters, planetary systems, and free-floating planets with direct N-body simulations. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Astronomisches Rechen-Institut, Zentrum fuer Astronomie, Heidelberg University.

My current work covers the DRAGON simulation series, planetary systems in star clusters, and NBODY6++GPU development. Most of it comes back to one question: what changes when planets, discs, stars, and compact objects evolve inside a crowded stellar system?
